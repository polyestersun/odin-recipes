Sweet Chilli Tofu Recipe

Ingredients for Tofu:

  • 1 Block Extra Firm Tofu
  • 1 tbs Soy Sauce
  • 2 tbs Cornstarch
  • 1/2 tbs Onion Powder
  • 1/2 tbs Garlic Powder
  • Sald & Black Pepper
  • 2 tbs Olive Oil

Ingredients for Sweet Chilli:

  • 1/4 cup Vegan Friendly Sweet Chili Sauce
  • 2 tbs Low Sodium Soy Sauce
  • 1/2-1 teaspoon Vegan Friendly Sriracha
  • 2 tbs Rice Vinegar
  • 2 tbs Green Onions
  • 1 tbs Sesame Seeds

Steps:

  1. Press the block of tofu for at least 20 minutes to remove excess water
  2. Cut the tofu in to 16 large cubes or rectangles
  3. Toss the tofu in soy sauce until coated and then add the cornstarch, garlic, onion, salt and pepper. Toss carefully to coat.
  4. Heat a large pan on medium-high heat. Add 1-2 tablespoons vegetable oil or enough to coat the base of the pan and pan fry each side until golden brown, about 3-5 minutes each side.
  5. Whisk together the sweet chili sauce, soy sauce, sriracha, and rice vinegar in a small bowl.
  6. Thinly chop green onions and add to the sauce along with sesame seeds.
  7. Toss the tofu in to the sauce and serve with jasmine rice, vegetables of choice and top with extra green onions and more sesame seeds. Enjoy!
