Carbonara Recipe

Ingredients:

Spaghetti 150 g

2 rashes of smoked bacon

2 eggs

30 g Parmesan chees, plus extra for grating

Garlic and black pepper

What we need to do:

Cook pasta in boiling and salted water

Chop the bacon and cold pan over medium heat, add black pepper and garlic

Beat the eggs and add in grated parmesan, mix well

Turn off the heat, add pasta into the pan with bacon and splash little bit of pasta water into the pan

Pour egg and parmesan mix into the pant and mix well

Serve Carbonara on a plate and grate Parmesan on top
