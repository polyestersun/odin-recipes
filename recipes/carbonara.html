Carbonara Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti 150 g
  • 2 rashes of smoked bacon
  • 2 eggs
  • 30 g Parmesan cheese, plus extra for grating
  • Black pepper, garlic

Steps:

  1. Cook pasta in boiling and salted water
  2. Chop the bacon and add on a cold pan over medium heat, add garlic and black pepper
  3. Beat the eggs and add in grated Parmesan, mix well
  4. Turn off the heat, add pasta into the pan with bacon and splash little bit of pasta water into the pan
  5. Pour egg and parmesan mix into the pan and mix well
  6. Serve Carbonara on a plate, grate parmesan on top
