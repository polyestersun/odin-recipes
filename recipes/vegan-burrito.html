Vegan Burrito Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 large tortilla wraps
  • 2 large handfuls spinach leaves, shredded
  • 1 avocado, thinly sliced (optional)
  • hot sauce, to serve

Ingredients for Chipotle Black Beans:

  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • 1 tbsp chipotle paste
  • 400g can chopped tomatoes
  • 400g black beans, drained
  • 1 bunch coriander, chopped

Ingredients for Lime and Red Onion Rice:

  • 250g wholegrain rice, cooked and drained
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Â½ red onion, very finely chopped
  • 50g hazelnuts, roughly chopped

Steps:

  1. To make the beans, heat the oil in a pan and fry the garlic for a minute, then stir in the chipotle paste.
  2. Tip in the tomatoes, stir and bring to a simmer. Season with salt. Simmer until thick, add the beans and cook briefly (make sure any water gets cooked off), then stir in the coriander.
  3. If you are using cold cooked rice, then warm it through, stir in the lime juice, red onion and nuts and season well.
  4. Lay out the tortillas and sprinkle over some spinach, add some avocado slices and some rice, then top with the bean mix.
  5. Roll the bottom up, then fold the sides in to stop the filling falling out as you roll. Wrap tightly in foil, if you like, and cut in half.
